    About Us

    DRSMinds is a global IT Consulting, Outsourcing, and Recruitment company leading innovative and modern IT staffing services to provide IT professionals for contract, temporary or permanent positions, in person or remote. Our dedicated and focused sourcing management team is committed to shortening hiring times to provide top-notch IT professionals.

    We are professional technology recruiters, and We have ambitious plans to build the #1 recruitment company dedicated to startups and software product companies. Headquartered in California, our global footprint includes offices in India and Poland.

    We're a fast-growing company with opportunities in many tech-related areas. The IT Industries inspire us with exponential potentials, such as Digital Transformation, Artificial Intelligence, Big Data, BI, Robotics & Autonomous Driving, and we aim to build relationships with businesses and candidates with that same vision.
